Study in the USA

The USA offers world-renowned education, vibrant campuses, and cutting-edge research opportunities.

  • Top Programs: Computer Science, Business, Engineering, Health Sciences
  • Popular Visa: F-1 Student Visa
  • Work Options: OPT for up to 3 years in STEM fields
  • Top Universities: MIT, Stanford, Harvard, UC Berkeley

Study in Canada

Canada is known for affordable tuition, multicultural cities, and a high quality of life.

  • Top Programs: AI, Cybersecurity, Engineering, Business
  • Popular Visa: Study Permit + PGWP
  • Work Options: Post-Graduate Work Permit (up to 3 years)
  • Top Universities: UofT, McGill, UBC, Waterloo

Study in Australia

Australia combines academic excellence with a relaxed lifestyle and beautiful landscapes.

  • Top Programs: Nursing, IT, Business, Environmental Science
  • Popular Visa: Subclass 500 Student Visa
  • Work Options: 2–4 year post-study work visa
  • Top Universities: University of Melbourne, ANU, University of Sydney

Study in the UK

The UK offers historic institutions, strong global rankings, and 1-year master's programs.

  • Top Programs: Business, Law, Engineering, Healthcare
  • Popular Visa: Student Route (Tier 4)
  • Work Options: Graduate Route - 2 years post-study
  • Top Universities: Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial College London

Study in Europe

Europe offers affordable tuition, diverse cultures, and a wide range of English-taught programs.

  • Top Programs: Social Sciences, Engineering, Design, International Relations
  • Popular Destinations: Germany, Netherlands, France, Sweden
  • Work Options: Job-seeker visas post-study (varies by country)
  • Top Universities: University of Amsterdam, LMU Munich, Sorbonne
